About PugChamp

the world's best PUG service

The Basics

MixChamp was conceived as a successor to tf2mix, a similar mix PUG system. It was also created as a pug system that is friendly to players that are new to pugs and PugChamp.

After developing PugChamp, we realized that there were a few barriers to entry in a high level pug system. We decided to remove captains and auto-generate teams to have quick, accessible pugs for all players of any skill level. Currently, MixChamp is in Alpha and will be developed alongside PugChamp.

Open Source

The base PugChamp system is fully open source - help contribute to it or use it in your own project!
